Smashburger

Description:

This is a classic pan seared burger pressed flat until the edges turn crispy love story. Almost no prep time or cook time, this recipe will show you how to make mouth watering, smasheburgers that the whole family will be sure to love.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces of hamburger meat divided into 2 ounce balls
  • Condiments and toppings such as cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, ketchup, mayo, mustard etc.
  • Hamburger buns
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare burger bun by laying toppings on bottom half of bun. Have it nearby and ready for when your burger is cooked.
  2. Preheat a large stainless steel saut√© pan or skillet over high heat for 2 minutes. Place balls of beef in pan and smash down with a stiff metal spatula, using a second spatula to add pressure. Smashed patties should be slightly wider than burger bun.
  3. Season generously with salt and pepper and allow to cook until patties are well browned and tops are beginning to turn pale pink/gray in spots, about 45 seconds. Using a bench scraper or the back side of a stiff metal spatula, carefully scrape patties from pan, making sure to get all of the browned bits.
  4. Flip patties and immediately place a slice of cheese over 1 patty, then stack the second directly on top. Immediately remove from pan and transfer to waiting burger bun. Serve.
